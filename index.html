What is a Claw Machine?

If this is your first time learning about claw machines, you don't have to worry because there are many people who don't know as well. Claw Machine's come in different varieties and style. The standard one that we use in our store is called the "Three prong" claw machine, otherwise known as a skill crane or claw crane. There are many claw machines out there that are different and are much simplier than this one (see picture to left). We hope to incorporate more machines like 2 prong UFO Catchers and other Japanese style claw machines. Tips and Guides

In order to be good or great at clawing your prize, there are some things you must keep in mind:
1) Maintain a strong mindset in winning your prize
2) Watch the middle point of the claw and position that to the center of the prize.
3) Don't expect to get it on the first try, it will take a couple times before the claw is warmed up.
